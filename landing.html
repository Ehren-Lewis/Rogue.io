What is Cyber Interactive?

  Cyber Interactive is a RPG-style Social Media application designed to bring you any and all needs you may need elsewhere. It is designed to be layered on top of reality, but isn't designed to be seperate from it.

Quantify Your Life

  Qualia is the hidden trait that seperates man from machine. Growing up in the information age, We have all been accustomed to the idea of leveling, skill trees, and quests through videogames that populate our society. Cyber Interactive is determined to turn the Qualia into quantity to give tried and true feedback of the nature that makes each individual who they are.
